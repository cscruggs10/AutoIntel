## 1. i FINANCE LLC (d/b/a DEAL MACHINE)

**What it is:** Wholesale auto operation and service shop  
**Legal entity:** i Finance LLC  
**DBA/Brand:** Deal Machine  
**Location:** Memphis, TN  
**Two complementary verticals (separate P&Ls):**

### A. Wholesale Auto
- Buy used trade-ins from dealer network
- Aggregate at UAX Memphis (weekly auctions, Wednesdays)
- Sell at public auctions across TN, MS, AL (~2x monthly)
- Unique offering: Point-of-sale title loan financing up to $2,500 at auction
- Typical loan: $1,700, 12 months, 25% monthly (279% APR)
- ~$800 margin per vehicle + interest income
- 30% default rate but losses near zero due to collateral recovery

### B. Service Shop
- Operates as separate P&L from wholesale
- Handles reconditioning, inspections, mechanical work
- Complements wholesale by prepping inventory and servicing dealer clients
- Key personnel: Steve (inspections/estimates), Cameron (operations/communications)
- Tools: Rapid Recon (video documentation)

**Website:** dealerdealmachine.com
